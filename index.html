I am currently a Postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Economics and Management "Marco Fanno" at the University of Padova.

I am involved in a project about "The political economy of reforms and the costs of populism" (PERCOP).

I hold Ph.D. in Economics from Nova School of Business and Economics.

I am affiliated with the Economics for Policy Knowledge Center  

I am an applied microeconomist with research interests in political economy. I am particularly interested in studying contemporaneous topics relevant to liberal democracies and their core values.

My empirical work combines causal inference methods with several types of data (administrative, survey or text data).

Research field: Political Economy

Research methods: Causal inference, Natural language processing
